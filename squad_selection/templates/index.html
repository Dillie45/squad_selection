Project Overview: Selecting the Best Cricket Squad

This project is dedicated to selecting the best cricket squad, including a core eleven players and a few extra players, from a pool of Indian cricketers based on their performance over the last decade. By analyzing recent data such as batting averages, bowling figures, strike rates, and overall contributions across different formats, we aim to build a well-rounded and competitive team
